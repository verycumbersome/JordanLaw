Our mission statement

At Jordan Law, P.C. we want to build lasting relationships with our clients. We are a small general practice firm that seeks to distinguish itself with outstanding service to clients and dedication to our community. Attorney Susan Ezzy Jordan has more than 20 years of experience practicing law in Lane County in a wide range of practice areas, including Estate Planning and Administration, Juvenile Law, Criminal Law, Civil Litigation, and Real Property matters and disputes.

As an attorney living and working in Eugene since 1997, Susan has acquired broad experience in the practice of law and general practice issues. Susan provides flat-fee consultations for clients seeking advice about litigation issues and other legal matters, including contract and document review.
