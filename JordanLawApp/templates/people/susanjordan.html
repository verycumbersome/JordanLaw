About Me

Hows it going? My name is Matthew Jordan and thanks for stopping by my website. This is a place to pretty much keep tabs on who I am and what im working on.

My first program I wrote was in 3rd grade on the Blender 3D engine to make a math-based video game for a school project. I used python exclusively for the first 5 years of my programming experience and to this day it remains one of my favorite languages. A few years later I had moved on from 3D games and onto web development.

My first web development project I worked on was for my friend's music collective. The project involved port forewarding an Apache web server on a dinky 2009 mac mini running and old version of Snow Leopard. While working on the site, I learned about new languages and software such as HTML, CSS, PHP, and Apache. This early experience proved pivotal to my early love of web dev.

After graduating into highschool, I discovered the robotics team and quickly joined. While on my team, I not only learned new languages but how to apply them as well. The languages and libraries I learned/improved on during my time there were Java, Javascript, Python, and OpenCv.
