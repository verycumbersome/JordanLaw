Susan Ezzy Jordan

Attorney at Law

E susanjordan.attorney@gmail.com
T 541-554-7684 | F fax-number-here
VCARD

Susan graduated from the University of Maine in 1990 with a Bachelor of Sciences degree in Journalism, and obtained her law degree from the University of Oregon School of Law in 1997. During law school she worked as a Judicial Extern in the United States District Court for the District of Oregon, was a summer associate for a litigation firm in Oregon, and worked as a research assistant in the area of Contract Law. She was a Staff Editor on the Journal of Environmental Law and Litigation (JELL), a member of the law student organization, L.A.W. (Land, Air, Water) and played on the City League law school women’s soccer team.
---
After graduating from the University of Oregon School of Law, Susan clerked for three Lane County Circuit Court judges and then joined a Springfield firm where she practiced for three years, representing the City of Springfield and practicing in the area of civil litigation, with a focus on real property disputes. Susan left the firm and worked briefly for the University of Oregon School of Law before opening her own practice in 2005, originally Susan Ezzy Jordan, LLC, now Jordan Law, P.C.
---
Susan’s background includes general civil litigation and exposure to a wide range of practice areas including Civil Litigation, Estate Planning, Family Law and matters involving Real Property. In 2007, she began practicing Juvenile Law and became an active member of the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium. Juvenile Law constitutes a significant portion of her practice and Susan also continues to assist clients with other issues related to her general practice areas.
---
Jordan Law continues to grow and expand our practice areas to meet our clients’ needs. We also maintain relationships with other attorneys in Lane County and will refer clients when referrals are appropriate to best suit their needs.
